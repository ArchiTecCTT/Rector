# Code of Conduct

Rector follows a concise Contributor Covenant-style code of conduct.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity or orientation, or technology choices.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept responsibility for impact.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing private information without permission.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith arguments.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make the community unsafe or unwelcoming.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban participants for behavior that violates this code.

To report concerns privately, please contact the project maintainers through the temporary reporting channel described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) (such as a GitHub Private Security Advisory or direct maintainer contact), rather than opening a public issue. Maintainers should review reports promptly, fairly, and with respect for reporter privacy.
